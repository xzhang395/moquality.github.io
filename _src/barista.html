Barista

Making automated tests for your mobile app.


Want to rapidly build an automated test suite?
Barista lets you build your automated tests right on your device.
All you need to do is interact with your app like you normally would and tell barista what you'd like to check.

Download for Android
Interested in iOS version?

How does it work?

Barista records your interactions with the application under test and adds them to your test case. On Android, it leverages the accessibility mechanisms so that you don't need to modify your app before you use Barista.

At any point during the test, you can use Barista's visual tool (shown in the interactive image) to add custom assertions on widgets on the screen or to take a screenshot. The assertions presented are based on the type of widget (i.e., Text widgets will have textual assertions available).

Once you have created the test, Barista will send it over to you by email. You can also get the tests from our website or directly inside your Android project using our gradle plugin.
