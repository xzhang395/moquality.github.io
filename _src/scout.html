Scout

Helping you create better beta test reports.


Want to focus more on exploratory testing and less on tracking screenshots?
Scout captures your test session in the background while you're exploring the app.
The captured session contains screenshots, performance statistics and more. You can easily see this information on your computer and share it with others on your team!

How does it work?

As a app developer, you can get more from your beta testers by using Scout for your beta testing. On Android, you do not need to change anything in your app as Scout leverages the underlying system APIs to capture everything about your app.

With Scout, you can get videos and screenshots of your beta test sessions along with system performance details. Finally, you can find the bottlenecks in your app and measure how it impacts usage!
